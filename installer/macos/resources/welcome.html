Welcome to NoiseAI

NoiseAI removes background noise from your microphone in real time using AI-powered audio processing.

This installer will set up:

  • NoiseAI.app — the menu bar application that captures and denoises your audio
  • NoiseAI Audio Driver — a virtual microphone that other apps can use as their input device

After installation, launch NoiseAI from your Applications folder and select NoiseAI Microphone as the input device in any app (Zoom, Teams, FaceTime, etc.).

Note: The installer requires administrator privileges to install the audio driver.
